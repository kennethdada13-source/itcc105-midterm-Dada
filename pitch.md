ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot – Agile Pitch

 Problem Statement

The current student portal is confusing and time-consuming because students must navigate many menus to access simple information.

 Industry 5.0 Justification

The AI Copilot helps students by providing instant answers and guidance, making tasks faster and easier while supporting—not replacing—users.

User Stories

1. As a student, I want to check my clearance using AI, so that I can save time.
2. As a student, I want to view my schedule quickly, so that I avoid navigating menus.
3. As a student, I want AI reminders, so that I stay updated on deadlines.
